Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a safe and anonymous platform for individuals to unburden their hearts and minds by sharing their untold stories of abuse and trauma. Through the power of expressive writing, we aim to foster emotional healing, build a supportive community, and raise awareness about the profound impact of abuse. We believe in the strength of shared experiences and the transformative power of words to bring comfort, understanding, and hope to those who need it most.

Our Story

Emails I Never Sent began with a simple yet profound idea: to create a space where individuals could express their deepest thoughts and experiences without fear of judgment. We are dedicated to helping people find their voice and reclaim their narrative through the therapeutic power of writing.

About the Founder

Alaina W is the founder of "Emails I Never Sent". After overcoming her own experiences with sexual abuse, Jane was inspired to create a platform where survivors can share their stories in a safe and supportive environment. With a background in social work and advocacy, Jane has dedicated her life to helping others find their voice and heal from trauma. Her mission is to provide a space where individuals can express their unsent messages, find community, and begin their journey towards healing.
